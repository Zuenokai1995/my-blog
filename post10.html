Day 16: Witherwood (2/5 areas) done

That’s right — Witherwood is surprisingly bigger than the village (obviously, I know).
Took me over 16 hours of straight pixel drawing. Glad my imagination power came in handy — helps me visualize ideas well without drifting into meaningless adventures.

Every corner of Witherwood is starting to come alive now. From gnarled twisted trees to icy mist patches, it feels like I’m sculpting a real breathing forest bit by bit. Two zones are completely structured — only three more to go. It’s starting to feel huge.

I honestly didn’t expect the forest to take shape so vividly. Guess it’s the late-night focus + snowy outside vibes combo that hit different.

That’s it for today. Off to sleep with frost still in my head.
