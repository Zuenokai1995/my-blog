Farewell, Ashbound

After much thought and consideration, I've decided to discontinue the Ashbound project. While it began with immense passion, vision, and creativity, I’ve grown in a different direction since then.

Ashbound helped me sharpen my ideas, explore game development, and understand what kind of stories and systems I truly want to build. But it's time to close this chapter so I can pursue something that aligns better with who I am now.

For those who followed or supported the project: thank you. Your encouragement and engagement meant a lot. This isn’t the end of my creative journey—just the end of this particular path.

Until next time, stay curious and keep building.
